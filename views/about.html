About

Athlone Kids Camps

Summer Camps!! The hottest topic on (almost) every parent's lips as soon as Summer Term hits! We start scratching our heads and anxiety almost hits us when we anticipate the Summer Holidays coming up - and dread fills us when we try to think about how we will fill the void in the glorious two months of the Irish school holidays for our wonderful offspring! But getting the right camps booked in for children can become daunting and frustrating especially if you're not completely in the "know" of what goes on in your locality, leading to missing out on popular camps.

Because Summer Camps are always in high demand, especially with the Irish Weather in recent years, we know that it can be very frustrating to find a camp that manages that magical combination of being suitable for your child and not too far away, not to mention reasonably priced. Thankfully there is a great variety of camps on offer for kids and teens for the summer holidays, from sports camps, activity camps, arts and crafts camps and lots more. And all local too in the Athlone area.

We've done the research for you and here we have compiled the most popular Kids Camps in the Athlone area that may otherwise have escaped from your radar! Athlone Kids Camps is a one-stop shop for all your camps in your local area. Athlone Kids Camps aims to be your go-to website and WebApp resource centre for Kids Camps available in and around the Athlone area. Ranging from Multi-Activity Camps, Sports Camps, Performance Arts Camps, Computer and STEM Camps, Outdoor Adventure Camps, Teen Camps and more - you will find one that's suitable for your child.

We will be continually adding to the list of camps as they become available, do please subscribe to our free Newsletter for information and ideas on available kids each school break. We also welcome any Kids Camp recommendations that you might have - do chat to us on our contact page and we will feature your recommendations. We also welcome any feedback that you might have - on your favourite camps and also those that may need to be improved or expanded on!

Athlone Kids Camps - For all your Local Kids Camps!

Your comprehensive guide to the best and most popular Kids Camps in and around your local Athlone area!

Multi-Activity Camps

Multi-Activity camps provide "taster" or introductory sessions (about 4 -6 a day) in a range of different activities.

Activities such as sports, adventure, creative and performing arts are sampled for the enjoyment of each participant.

Sports Camps

The sports camps are usually single activity sports camps led by qualified Instructors / Coaches / Associations.

Sports camps include Athletics, GAA, Soccer and more. Examples: FAI Soccer Camp, Kellogg's GAA Cul Camps etc.

Performance Arts Camps

Performance Arts Camps can include Drama, Singing, Dancing and Music, or possibly a combination of all of these.

Given by schools such as Athlone School of Music, Speech and Drama, Ballet and Modern and Irish Dancing schools.

Computer and STEM Camps

Tech camps include all the trade tools for your budding tech rockstar to pick up valuable tech and STEM skills!

Designed for children of all abilities and includes WeVideo, Soundtrap, Lego, BBC Micro Bit and more...

Outdoor Adventure Camps

Outdoor adventure camps are mostly run by specialist centres catering to specialist outdoor pursuits.

Camps usually include a range of outdoor sports such as kayaking, sailing, rock climbing, abseiling etc...

Teen Camps

Teen camps help teenagers make the most of their interests to acquire great new skills of their choosing.

Students leave with an increased sense of confidence they can use long after the Summer Camp has finished.
