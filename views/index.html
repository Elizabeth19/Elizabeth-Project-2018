Athlone Kids Camps - For all your Local Kids Camps!

Your comprehensive guide to the best and most popular Kids Camps in and around your local Athlone area!

Multi-Activity Camps

Multi-Activity camps provide "taster" or introductory sessions (about 4 -6 a day) in a range of different activities.

Activities such as sports, adventure, creative and performing arts are sampled for the enjoyment of each participant.

Sports Camps

The sports camps are usually single activity sports camps led by qualified Instructors / Coaches / Associations.

Sports camps include Athletics, GAA, Soccer and more. Examples: FAI Soccer Camp, Kellogg's GAA Cul Camps etc.

Performance Arts Camps

Performance Arts Camps can include Drama, Singing, Dancing and Music, or possibly a combination of all of these.

Given by schools such as Athlone School of Music, Speech and Drama, Ballet and Modern and Irish Dancing schools.

Computer and STEM Camps

Tech camps include all the trade tools for your budding tech rockstar to pick up valuable tech and STEM skills!

Designed for children of all abilities and includes WeVideo, Soundtrap, Lego, BBC Micro Bit and more...

Outdoor Adventure Camps

Outdoor adventure camps are mostly run by specialist centres catering to specialist outdoor pursuits.

Camps usually include a range of outdoor sports such as kayaking, sailing, rock climbing, abseiling etc...

Teen Camps

Teen camps help teenagers make the most of their interests to acquire great new skills of their choosing.

Students leave with an increased sense of confidence they can use long after the Summer Camp has finished.
